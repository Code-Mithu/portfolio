# Portfolio Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## Project Overview
This project aims to build a modern, responsive personal portfolio website to establish and enhance professional presence. The site will act as the single source of truth for career achievements, skills, and projects, enabling networking, job seeking, and professional brand management.

## Business Goals
1. Increase professional visibility among recruiters and industry peers.
2. Provide a compelling, accessible platform for showcasing project experience and skill depth.
3. Facilitate seamless contact and connection requests from potential collaborators or employers.
4. Establish a consistent personal brand identity across the professional web.

## User Personas
- **The Recruiter:** Looking for quick access to resume, core skills, and notable project achievements. Needs clean navigation and clear value propositions.
- **The Potential Client/Collaborator:** Interested in understanding depth of expertise, specific problem-solving examples, and personal work style/approach.
- **The Peer/Mentor:** Interested in career trajectory, technical insights, and keeping up to date with professional activities.

## Functional Requirements
- **Contact:** Secure contact form submission.
- **Projects Showcase:** Categorized, searchable, and filterable view of key project work.
- **Resume Access:** Downloadable PDF version and embedded text representation.
- **Responsive Design:** Consistent user experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
- **Social/Networking Links:** Direct links to GitHub, LinkedIn, and other relevant profiles.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- **Security:** Use HTTPS; sanitize all contact form inputs to prevent injection attacks.
- **Maintainability:** Modular structure to allow easy content updates (skills, experience, projects).
- **Scalability:** Capable of handling increased traffic during job searches or public awareness campaigns.

## Page Requirements
- **Home:** Hero introduction, summary of value, quick links to Projects and Contact.
- **About:** Professional story, values, approach.
- **Skills:** Categorized technical and soft skills.
- **Projects:** List view with details/case studies for each.
- **Experience:** Chronological history of roles and achievements.
- **Education:** Academic background and certifications.
- **Resume:** Viewable and downloadable document.
- **Contact:** Contact form and professional social links.

## Content Requirements
- Professional biography.
- High-quality project summaries (Problem, Solution, Impact).
- Clearly defined skill taxonomy.
- Updated professional achievements.
- Professional portrait or representative imagery.

## SEO Requirements
- Proper use of semantic HTML tags (H1-H6).
- Meaningful meta descriptions and title tags for every page.
- Semantic URLs (e.g., `/projects`, `/about`).
- Fast page loading times (optimized images, minified assets).

## Accessibility Requirements
- Adherence to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards.
- Proper contrast ratios for text.
- Screen reader accessibility (semantic structure, alt text).
- Keyboard-only navigation capability.

## Performance Requirements
- Target < 2s load time on broadband.
- Optimized performance for mobile devices.
- Efficient caching strategy.

## MVP Scope
- Core pages: Home, About, Skills, Projects, Experience, Education, Contact.
- Basic Resume page.
- Functioning contact form.
- Mobile responsiveness.
- Essential SEO meta-tags.

## Future Scope
- Integrated Blog for professional insights.
- Advanced project filtering and search.
- Interactive data visualizations for skills.
- Internationalization support.
- API-driven dynamic content updates.
