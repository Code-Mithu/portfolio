# Content Improvement Report

## 1. Overview
This report provides a critique of the current portfolio website content. Overall, the structure is professional, but the content requires further refinement to stand out to recruiters and hiring managers.

## 2. Section Analysis

| Section | Status | Observations |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **About** | Fair | Solid foundational structure, but the content relies on generic professional summaries. |
| **Experience** | Good | Responsibilities are clearly listed, but impact metrics are limited. |
| **Skills** | Good | Well-categorized and easy to read. |
| **Projects** | Fair | Descriptions are concise, but lack depth regarding technical challenges and outcomes. Thumbnails are placeholders. |
| **Resume** | Good | Professional summary and certifications are clearly presented. |

## 3. Key Findings & Improvement Recommendations
- **Impact Metrics**: The experience section lists tasks rather than outcomes. Shift focus from "What I did" to "What was the business value/impact" using metrics (%, $, time saved).
- **Project Narrative**: Projects lack context. Move from "What it is" to "Why I built it, what technical challenge I solved, and what the final result was."
- **Visuals**: Replace generic Unsplash thumbnails with actual project screenshots or professional mockups to build credibility.
