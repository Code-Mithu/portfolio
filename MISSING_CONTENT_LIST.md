# Missing Content List

The following content is recommended for addition to enhance the portfolio's credibility and recruiter engagement:

- [ ] **Testimonials**: Quotes from past clients, colleagues, or managers vouching for your technical skills and collaboration style.
- [ ] **Case Studies / Blog**: Detailed technical articles or case studies demonstrating problem-solving ability, decision-making processes, and technical depth.
- [ ] **Social Proof**: Links to specific technical contributions (e.g., Pull Requests on popular open-source projects, talk recordings, or technical blog posts).
- [ ] **Direct Contact Information**: While present in the footer, adding a summary of preferred contact methods (or a brief "Let's connect" call to action) within the Contact section itself improves user experience.
