# Wireframes (WIREFRAMES.md)

## 1. Home
*   **Structure:** Hero Section (Intro/CTA), Featured Projects Preview, Quick Links (About/Contact).
*   **Order:** Hero, Featured Projects, About Preview, Quick Contact CTA.

## 2. About
*   **Structure:** Biography Section, Values/Philosophy.
*   **Order:** Portrait/Bio, Values, Skills (Brief), Education (Brief).

## 3. Skills
*   **Structure:** Categorized grid of skills (Technical, Soft).
*   **Order:** Technical Skills Grid, Soft Skills List, CTA (Projects).

## 4. Projects
*   **Structure:** Filter/Category Bar, Grid/List of Projects (Card-based).
*   **Order:** Filter Bar, Project Cards (Title, Tech Stack, Brief Description, Link).

## 5. Experience
*   **Structure:** Chronological timeline.
*   **Order:** Role Details (Company, Role, Dates, Key Achievements).

## 6. Education
*   **Structure:** List of institutions/certifications.
*   **Order:** Institution, Degree/Certificate, Dates.

## 7. Resume
*   **Structure:** Embedded PDF viewer + Download button.
*   **Order:** Download CTA, Embedded Viewer.

## 8. Contact
*   **Structure:** Contact Form (Name, Email, Message), Social Links.
*   **Order:** Form, Contact Information/Social Links.
